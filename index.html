About Me

I am a final year PhD student in the Rice Computational Imaging Lab led by Prof. Ashok Veeraraghavan at Rice University, Houston TX USA.

My research interests lie at the intersection of vision, sensing, optics and machine learning.

I am on the 2025 job market, happy to connect and talk about opportunities. I am seeking full-time opportunities that are based in India, US, or elsewhere. Here is my resume.

My Interests

I am interested in developing methods for imaging systems or visual display systems using ideas from Image and Signal Processing, Computer Vision, Optics, and Deep Learning. I aspire to gain “full-stack knowledge” of imaging systems and use it for novel applications - and in the process solve sensing problems in healthcare, robotics, autonomous navigation, AR/VR, etc.
